Batting

  By: admin   Uncategorized   0 Comments   July 14, 2021

The player who bats nicely and can score runs are batsman. Batsman should hit the ball well and score as much as they can for their team. A batter can score run by running between the wickets, scoring boundaries ( sixes and fours).

Batting is one of the three main part of cricket. The player who does batting well they are called batters or batsman.T o be a good batsman there are many things to work about. They are

1) Stance

2) Grip

3)Feet Movement

4) Weight Transfer

5) Eye Contact

6) Reaction Time

7) Awareness about field setup
